Who we are

Alpha Rho Theta is a service fraternity that was founded on November 3rd 2016 at Virginia Polytechnic Institute. Our goal was to create an organization open to any and all student of Virginia tech who want to both further their leadership skills and resume while giving back to our blacksburg community. Our main mission is helping the homeless but we find it important to help out wherever and whenever we are able. Besides the volunteer work we do, Alpha Rho also engages in extracurricular social events. We believe fostering an all inclusive enviroment where members can get to know eachother and create lasting freindships is an important aspect in getting people out to participate.
